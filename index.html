Everybody A Learner

We envision schools where new and expansive patterns of thinking are nurtured, where collective aspiration is set free and where people are continually learning how to learn together.

Mission

To deeply engage with Utah charter schools as a strategic partner committed to achieving learning proficiency for every enrolled student. Our collaborative support for effective leadership and efficient charter school operations is animated by a passion for cultivating learning systems grounded in research and shown to increase learning proficiency in K-12 students.

Vision

Every student enrolled in a CLP affiliated Utah charter school will obtain the knowledge, skills and dispositions needed to become a confident and contributing adult in their chosen communities.

Purpose

Our purpose is to be an opportunity accelerator for Utah's children as they cultivate a portfolio of learning-assets foundational to the contributions they make to their communities as life-long learners. To fulfill this purpose, we will work as a strategic catalyst for Utah Charter Schools who share our commitment to continuous and sustainable improvement in student learning outcomes for all students.

How We Help

  • Charter School Authorization

    Clear Learning Partners is committed to improving student learning outcomes in Utah’s charter schools. Our team brings relevant experience and familiarity with Utah State University’s authorization process that you can leverage on your journey to create a charter school committed to student achievement.

  • School Leadership

    Leadership is critical to sustainable success within a charter school organization. Clear Learning Partners is deeply passionate about helping charter school leaders create and sustain a culture that supports student learning within every facet and function of a charter school. Our advisory services are designed to support information rich decision making processes that help the charter school leadership team—from the board of directors to the individual teachers—as they strive to create a school that will open a world of opportunity for students to have the confidence and agility to navigate an ever-changing and increasingly interconnected world.

  • Enterprise Management

    Bringing extensive expertise from both private and public sectors, our team can advise you and your staff as you integrate the business and academic sides of charter school operations in ways that further your charter school’s mission. Clear Learning Partners will walk alongside charter school leadership as a partner committed to supporting them as they direct resources to drive student achievement.

Emmy Heywood

Christopher Fawson

Dr. Fawson is a faculty member in the department of economics and finance at Utah State University where he teaches and conducts research in a variety of public policy fields—including school choice and charter school market structures. During his tenure at Utah State University, Dr. Fawson has served as Director of International Programs, Vice Provost for Academic and International Affairs, Department Head and Senior Associate Dean in the Huntsman School of Business. In 2013 he co-founded Strata Policy, a nonprofit research and learning-focused organization, that seeks to advance policy-relevant knowledge while cultivating the next generation of leadership talent in the broad area of liberty and free markets and designing and delivering learning innovations at the intersection of business ethics and entrepreneurship. Dr. Fawson maintains an active commitment to teaching and is passionate about designing and delivering transformational learning experiences for students. As a co-founder of Clear Learning Partners, Dr. Fawson is committed to helping Charter Schools create effective and efficient leadership and enterprise management systems that are aligned with a deeply-held commitment to improved learning outcomes for K-12 students.
